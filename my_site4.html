WHAT MAKES A GOOD FOOTBALL BALL?

Every football ball is engineered to offer the best performance for your game, and when coupled with a good pair of football boots, it can make you unstoppable. A good football ball has four important attributes: touch, how the ball feels when it connects with your foot; movement, how well the ball moves across the pitch; flight, how well it moves through the air when kicked aloft or thrown; and construction, how well the ball is held together, what it’s made of and how heavy it is. You should also consider how many ‘panels’ the ball has on its exterior, with a higher number generally making the football ball easier to control and a lower number providing greater speed.
